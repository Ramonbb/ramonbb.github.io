Biography

Academic Profile

Ramón Barrera-Barrera is a university professor and researcher specializing in Tourism and Marketing. His research examines how consumers evaluate tourism services, how online reviews shape destination and hospitality decisions, and how advanced quantitative and text-analysis methods can support tourism management.

This portal brings together publications, research projects, teaching activities, and interactive appendices designed to make research outputs transparent, reusable, and easy to explore.
